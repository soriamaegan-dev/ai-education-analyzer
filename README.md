## What Does It Do?

The AI Education Analyzer is a free, open-source AI-powered tool that helps school administrators, teachers, district officials, and educational researchers analyze student and institutional data in minutes. Upload your de-identified performance data, enrollment records, curriculum assessments, or budget files and the AI instantly generates comprehensive analysis including student performance trends, dropout risk scoring, curriculum effectiveness evaluation, enrollment forecasting, resource optimization, and professional reports. What used to take hours of manual analysis now takes minutes.

---

## 9 Powerful Tools in One App

### 📊 Data Upload & Overview
Upload CSV, Excel, PDF, or JSON educational files and get an instant AI-generated overview. Identifies key academic metrics, grade levels, subjects, time periods covered, data quality issues, and recommends the best analyses to run next.

### 📝 Student Performance Analysis
Upload de-identified student performance data and the AI identifies students performing below proficiency, analyzes subject area trends, compares grade levels, identifies learning gaps, and delivers an improvement trajectory analysis — with a color-coded performance chart showing students above, near, and below the proficiency threshold.

### 🚨 Dropout & Retention Analysis
Upload enrollment and attendance data and the AI calculates dropout risk scores, identifies at-risk indicators, analyzes attendance patterns including chronic absenteeism, recommends targeted interventions, identifies success factors, and delivers an early warning system design — with a risk distribution chart.

### 📚 Curriculum Effectiveness
Upload assessment and course data and the AI measures teaching effectiveness, analyzes course completion rates, reviews assessment score trends, identifies learning objective gaps, evaluates standards alignment, and delivers specific curriculum improvement recommendations with professional development priorities.

### 📈 Enrollment Forecasting
Upload enrollment history data and the AI generates 1, 3, and 5 year enrollment forecasts, analyzes demographic trends, identifies grade level capacity issues, assesses program popularity, forecasts revenue for private schools, and delivers staffing level recommendations — with an interactive trend and forecast chart.

### 💰 Resource & Budget Analysis
Upload budget and resource data and the AI calculates per-student costs, identifies over and under-funded areas, assesses technology utilization, analyzes budget variances, identifies cost saving opportunities, and highlights applicable federal and state funding and grant opportunities — with a budget allocation pie chart.

### 📉 Visualizations & Charts
Auto-generates interactive education analytics charts including bar charts, line charts, pie charts, scatter plots, heatmaps, box plots, histograms, area charts, and funnel charts. Select any columns to visualize using Plotly interactive charts.

### 📄 Reports & Presentations
Generates a full PowerPoint presentation, PDF school performance report, board of education summary, or annual report directly from your educational data. Professional education administration formatting with all AI insights included. Download with one click.

### 💬 AI Data Chat
Ask questions about your uploaded educational data in plain English. Get answers, SQL queries, Python code, absenteeism calculations, and deeper insights on demand. Conversation history maintained throughout your session.
